# Create meta/rules Document

## Quick Steps

1. Go to: https://console.firebase.google.com/project/apes-365b0/firestore/data
2. Click **"Start collection"** (if no collections exist) or the **"+"** button
3. **Collection ID:** `meta`
4. Click **"Next"**
5. **Document ID:** `rules` (toggle "Auto-ID" OFF and type manually)
6. Click **"Next"**
7. Add field:
   - **Field:** `version`
   - **Type:** string
   - **Value:** `2025-12-24-1400`
8. Click **"Save"**

Done! Now `meta/rules` will be readable and show the version.
